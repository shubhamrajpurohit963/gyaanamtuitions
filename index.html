Boards

State Board

Every state board has its own educational approach which causes a variance in the syllabus from one state to another state in India.

Objective:
It focuses on state level topics and content of local relevance which helps students in preparing for state level engineering and medical entrance tests.

CBSE board

The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) is the most popular board in India , controlled and managed by Union Government of India.

Objective:
CBSE always works with a vision to provide stress-free education to its students by adapting innovative teaching methods infused with psychological and principles.

CBSE Board

The Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE) is a private non- governmental board of school education in India.

Objective:
The main objective of ICSE is to provide high quality education to students through a practical approach. It encourages students to choose across diverse streams/subjects.
